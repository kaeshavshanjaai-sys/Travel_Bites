ABOUT NEW YORK

The Big Apple

New York City is the world's cultural capital — a city of towering ambition and incredible diversity. From the neon glow of Times Square to the green oasis of Central Park, NYC is endlessly fascinating.

Catch a Broadway show, visit world-class museums like the Met and MoMA, and feast on cuisines from every corner of the globe. Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge at sunset for unforgettable views.

Every neighborhood tells a different story — from the artsy vibes of SoHo to the historic charm of Greenwich Village and the bustling energy of Chinatown.
